# Fluctuation Theorems for Funding-Driven Markets
## Non-Equilibrium Thermodynamics of Cryptocurrency Perpetual Futures

### Abstract
We map non-equilibrium statistical mechanics onto perpetual futures markets.
The funding rate = thermodynamic driving force. We test Jarzynski equality
and Crooks theorem on 6+ years of Binance data (BTC, ETH, SOL).
